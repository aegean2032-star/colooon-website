Privacy

Privacy Policy / Limited Use disclosure

Last updated: June 7, 2026

Local storage

colooon stores saved highlights, notes, captured questions and answers, collections, tags, source anchors, and export preferences locally in your browser.

This release does not provide account sync or a cross-device cloud archive.

No colooon server upload for saved content

Saved conversation text, notes, questions, answers, collections, tags, and exported content are not sent to a colooon server.

Exports are generated locally by user action as Markdown, PDF through the browser print window, or images rendered in the browser.

Limited use

colooon only uses accessed ChatGPT page content to provide user-facing features: Color On, Capture QA, Colored Moments, Back to the Scene, TO CHAT, and export.

colooon does not sell user data.

colooon does not use saved conversation content for advertising.

colooon does not use saved conversation content for unrelated purposes.

User actions

You may choose to send text back to ChatGPT or export/share files yourself.

Contact

Questions about privacy? Email support@colooon.com.
